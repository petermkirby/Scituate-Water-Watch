Get tested

Request a free at-home water test

With five different supply sources feeding Scituate's system, a household-level test tells you more than any system-wide report can.

What to expect

No cost, no obligation. This is a volunteer-run community service, not a sales call.

A local volunteer follows up within a few days to schedule a convenient time.

We test for the categories that matter most given Scituate's mixed supply: bacteria, PFAS, and common disinfection byproducts.

You'll get a plain-language explanation of your results, and, if anything stands out, a rundown of reasonable next steps for your household.

Scituate Water Watch is an independent, volunteer-run community water quality initiative. We are not affiliated with the Town of Scituate or the Scituate Water Division. Data referenced on this site is drawn from public EPA and Massachusetts DEP records, cross-checked against multiple independent trackers where possible.
